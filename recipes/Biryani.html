Description

Biryani is a delicious rice dish peppered with scrumptious spices like saffron and cumin and further layered with spiced meat or veggie protein.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Spices
  • Curd

Steps

  1. Mix all the spices
  2. Bring a pot of water to a boil and parboil the rice. Drain and set aside
  3. Layer half of the rice, all of the chicken, and then the remaining rice on top. Add the finishing touches. Allow steam to develop, then lower the heat and let the flavors meld
