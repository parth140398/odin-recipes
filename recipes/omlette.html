Description

There’s nothing more filling, delicious and nutritious than a protein-packed omelet, any time of the day

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Bread
  • Salted Butter

Steps

  1. ver medium-high heat, sauté the vegetables and garlic in olive oil until soft, about 3-5 minutes depending on the vegetables you choose
  2. Begin by whisking the eggs vigorously until they are homogenous (uniform throughout).
  3. add the whisked eggs and swirl them in the pan until they are evenly distributed
  4. Once the omelette is mostly set (about 5-6 minutes), carefully flip it and turn off the heat.
